About Me

Before I found my way into web development, my career path centered around serving others in customer service and HR roles. As a developer, I now blend my people-oriented skills with my passion for creating clean, functional, and visually captivating websites. Driven by my Enneagram 1 personality, I bring a keen eye for detail and always seek to enhance processes, communication, and the overall user experience.

My expertise in website design and development continues to expand, with a focus on leveraging the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js). Along with a diverse set of skills and tools, I create captivating websites and interactive web apps that deliver amazing user experiences.

I live in Northwest OKC with my wife Amber where we raise our seven kids. I enjoy playing the guitar and learning how people and things work. We love a good board game (or video game), playing LEGO, the neighborhood pool in the summer, family movie nights, and being loud. Most of all, we strive to live our lives in apprenticeship to Jesus, embracing the fullness of life He has to offer.
