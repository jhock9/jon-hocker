About Me

I live in northwest OKC with my wife Amber, where we raise our seven kids. Before I found my way into web development, my career centered around serving others in customer service and HR roles. Now as a developer, I blend my people-oriented skills with a love for creating clean, functional, and visually captivating websites and web apps that deliver amazing user experiences.

While my work mostly focuses on frontend development, I’m beginning to expand into full stack development to broaden my skill set through the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js). I'm always up for learning how to use new technologies as the digital landscape evolves. Currently, I'm intrigued by the potential of web3, AI, and blockchain development, and how to incorporate them into our day-to-day in safe and healthy ways. I also like to dabble in creative coding and generative art with p5.js.

Outside of coding, I enjoy playing the guitar, learning how people and things work, and practicing karate. As a family, we love a good board game (or video game), playing LEGO, hanging out at the neighborhood pool, and family movie nights. Most of all, we strive to live our lives in apprenticeship to Jesus, embracing the fullness of life He has to offer.
